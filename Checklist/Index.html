KCC Loan Checklist (Collateral-Free up to ₹2 Lakh)

For: April 2025 | Purpose: Emergency liquidity without selling land

1. Documents to Carry

  • Aadhaar card (original + photocopy)
  • Land ownership proof (Jamabandi / Mutation / Registry copy)
  • Bank passbook or account details
  • 1–2 passport-sized photographs
  • Self-written declaration or crop receipts
  • PAN card (optional but helpful)

2. What to Say at the Bank

  • "I’m applying for a Kisan Credit Card loan under the revised ₹2 lakh RBI limit."
  • "This is urgent for seasonal working capital and legal expenses."
  • "As per RBI circular Jan 1, 2025, no collateral or margin should be required."
  • If delay: Ask for Branch Manager and explain medical/legal urgency.

3. Follow-Up Steps

  • Get Loan Application No. or receipt
  • Note officer's name and submission time
  • Ask expected approval timeline
  • Follow-up after 2 days if needed

4. Optional Support Notes

  • Mention NABARD reference if prompted
  • Offer to sign a declaration of use for farm/legal purposes only
Note: You are not requesting a favor. You are exercising your farmer rights for support under the current RBI and Government policies.
